Valorant Story







Valorant's story is set on a near-future Earth after a mysterious event called First Light altered the world, introducing a powerful energy source called Radianite, which grants superpowers to some people (Radiants) and fuels advanced technology, leading to conflicts between Earth's factions, particularly against "Mirror Earth" counterparts, with the covert Valorant Protocol forming to control the chaos and stop global threats, often involving dangerous Radianite devices.
